Jarad Comingdeer.

Illustrator, Designer, Developer.

I am a front-end web developer based in San Diego, California. I love making applications that look great, and work even better.

Priorities

  • Usability First: Web applications are tools first. If you can't use it, no amount of bells and whistles are going to fix it.
  • Flexible Development: Design changes and code will break. Developing flexible products ensures longevity and painless changes down the road.
  • Give It A Soul: Style and presentation go a long way. Setting the right tone enhances experiences and can be the difference between good and blow-your-pants-off awesome.

Details

  • 5+ years experience developing everything from static webpages to full-fledged mobile and desktop applications.
  • Bachelor of Science in Interactive Media Design from the Art Institute of California, San Diego. Yes, I went to one of those 'Art' schools.
  • Comprehensive Front-end Developer focused on iterative development, responsive web design, and drawing fun guys like this.
